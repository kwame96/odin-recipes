Ingredients

  • Ribeye Steak
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Fresh Rosemary & Thyme

Steps

  1. To begin, let the steak rest for 30 minutes to come to room temperature. Salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Next, in a medium sized skillet over medium high heat add the olive oil. Once it starts to smoke add the steaks to the skillet. Sear on all of the sides until they have a golden brown crust.
  3. Add the butter and let melt in the skillet. Add in the garlic and fresh sprigs.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium and let them cook until they reach the desired internal temperature. Lastly, spoon the butter over the steak while they are cooking.
